About The Sanctuary

Sundar Rajan is an author, technologist, and long-time practitioner of Advaita Vedānta, with a practice tracing back to a formative encounter at the College of Engineering Guindy in 1978. He leads the PMI Technology Forum and writes at the crossroads of AI, contemplative inquiry, and lived spiritual experience. This site gathers that work — writing, talks, playlists, and interactive experiments — in one place.
